What is URL Encoding?

URL encoding converts special characters into a format that can be safely sent over the Internet. For example, a space becomes %20.

Why Encode?

  • URLs can't contain spaces or special characters like & and =.
  • Encoding ensures data is transmitted correctly.

Example

Before Encoding:
https://example.com/search?q=hello world & food

After Encoding:
https://example.com/search?q=hello%20world%20%26%20food
